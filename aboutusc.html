about us

welcome to the egyptian treasures website

Our mission is to inspire and educate visitors about Egyptian culture through meticulously sculpted historical artifacts preserved by Egypt's museums. Each museum has its own unique theme and rich history. When you explore our website, you'll discover seven outstanding museums:

  1. The Grand Egyptian Museum: A showcase of ancient Egyptian art, artifacts, and history.
  2. Cairo International Airport Museum: An introduction to Egypt's cultural heritage for travelers arriving in Cairo.
  3. National Museum of Egyptian Civilization: A comprehensive collection spanning Egypt's long history, from prehistoric times to the present day.
  4. The Egyptian Museum: Home to iconic treasures like the golden mask of Tutankhamun.
  5. Sharm El Sheikh Museum: A regional museum highlighting the Red Sea's natural beauty and historical significance.
  6. Coptic Museum:the largest collection of Coptic Christian artifacts in the world.The museum traces the history of Egypt from its beginnings to the present day.

Whether you're a family with kids or a tourist visiting Egypt, our website offers content tailored to your interests. Explore the wonders of Egypt's past and immerse yourself in its captivating stories.
